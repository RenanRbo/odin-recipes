Omelet

Description

Here's a super simple Omelet recipe:

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter (or oil)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional fillings (choose any or mix):
    • Shredded cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, etc.)
    • Chopped veggies (tomato, bell pepper, spinach, onions)
    • Cooked bacon or ham
    • Fresh herbs (parsley, chives, etc.)

Steps

  1. Prep the eggs: Crack the eggs into a bowl, add a pinch of salt and pepper, and whisk them together until the yolks and whites are fully combined.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick skillet or frying pan on medium heat and melt the butter (or heat the oil) until it's hot but not browning.
  3. Cook the eggs: Pour the eggs into the pan, swirling it around to spread them evenly. Let them cook for about 1-2 minutes until the edges start to set.
  4. Add fillings: Once the eggs start to set but the center is still slightly runny, sprinkle your fillings over half of the omelet.
  5. Fold and finish: Using a spatula, gently fold the omelet in half over the fillings. Let it cook for another 30 seconds to 1 minute to melt the cheese and ensure everything is heated through.
  6. Serve: Slide the omelet onto a plate and enjoy immediately!
