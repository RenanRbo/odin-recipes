Brazilian Cheese Bread

Description

Here's a classic Brazilian Cheese Bread recipe, naturally gluten-free and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (250g) tapioca flour
  • 1 cup (240ml) milk
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) vegetable oil (or butter)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 to 1½ cups (100-150g) grated cheese (traditionally Minas cheese, but mozzarella, parmesan, or a combo works well)
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine: Milk, Oil, Salt and Bring just to a boil, then remove from heat.
  3. Pour the hot mixture over the tapioca flour in a mixing bowl. Stir until combined - it will look gummy.
  4. Let the mixture cool slightly (about 5 minutes), then mix in the egg until smooth.
  5. Add the cheese and mix well. The dough should be sticky and stretchy. If it's too dry, add a splash of milk.
  6. With oiled hands or a cookie scoop, form small balls (about 1-1.5 inches in diameter) and place on the baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until puffed and golden.
